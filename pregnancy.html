First Trimester

  • Folic Acid-rich foods (leafy greens, citrus fruits, beans)
  • Protein sources (eggs, fish, lean meats, tofu)
  • Iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, fortified cereals)
  • Plenty of hydration (water, fresh juices)

Second Trimester

  • Calcium sources (dairy products, almonds, sardines)
  • Omega-3 fatty acids (salmon, walnuts, flaxseeds)
  • Vitamin D (fortified milk, egg yolks, sunlight exposure)
  • Fiber-rich foods (whole grains, fruits, vegetables)

Third Trimester

  • Increased protein intake (chicken, beans, Greek yogurt)
  • Iron and Vitamin C combination (lean meats with citrus fruits)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts)
  • Small, frequent meals to ease digestion

General Guidelines

  • Avoid processed and junk foods
  • Limit caffeine intake
  • Stay hydrated
  • Eat balanced meals with all essential nutrients
