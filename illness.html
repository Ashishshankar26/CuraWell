Cold & Flu

  • Warm soups (chicken broth, vegetable soup)
  • Herbal teas (ginger, turmeric, honey)
  • Vitamin C-rich foods (oranges, kiwis, bell peppers)
  • Hydrating fluids (water, coconut water)
  • Light, easy-to-digest foods (oatmeal, toast, yogurt)

Fever

  • Hydrating foods (cucumber, watermelon, coconut water)
  • Soft, easy-to-digest meals (boiled rice, mashed potatoes, soups)
  • Protein sources (boiled eggs, lentils, chicken)
  • Herbal teas (ginger tea, tulsi tea)

Stomach Flu / Food Poisoning

  • BRAT diet (Bananas, Rice, Applesauce, Toast)
  • Plain yogurt (probiotics to restore gut health)
  • Electrolyte-rich drinks (coconut water, ORS solution)
  • Light soups (vegetable or chicken broth)

Diarrhea

  • White rice, plain toast, boiled potatoes
  • Lean proteins (boiled chicken, fish, eggs)
  • Bananas and applesauce (to firm up stools)
  • Plenty of water and electrolyte drinks

Constipation

  • High-fiber foods (oats, lentils, flaxseeds)
  • Fresh fruits (papaya, prunes, pears)
  • Leafy greens and whole grains
  • Warm water with lemon in the morning

Sore Throat

  • Warm honey-lemon tea
  • Soft foods (mashed potatoes, oatmeal, yogurt)
  • Warm soups (chicken or vegetable broth)
  • Avoid spicy and acidic foods

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Low-fat dairy (Greek yogurt, skim milk)
  • Berries, bananas, and beets
  • Whole grains and nuts

Diabetes

  • Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa)
  • Fiber-rich foods (beans, lentils, vegetables)
  • Lean proteins (fish, chicken, tofu)
  • Low-sugar fruits (berries, apples)

Anemia (Iron Deficiency)

  • Iron-rich foods (spinach, red meat, beans, tofu)
  • Vitamin C sources (oranges, bell peppers)
  • Iron-fortified cereals and whole grains
  • Nuts and seeds (pumpkin seeds, flaxseeds)

Post-Surgery Recovery

  • High-protein foods (eggs, chicken, fish, lentils)
  • Hydrating foods (soups, coconut water)
  • Vitamin-rich foods (oranges, carrots, leafy greens)
  • Whole grains for energy (brown rice, oats)
